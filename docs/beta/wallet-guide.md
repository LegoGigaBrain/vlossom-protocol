# Wallet & Payments Guide

## Understanding Your Smart Wallet

When you create a Vlossom account, we automatically create a **smart wallet** for you. This is your personal wallet on the blockchain that holds your USDC balance.

### What Makes It "Smart"?

1. **No Gas Fees**: We sponsor all transaction fees for you
2. **Email Login**: No seed phrases or crypto wallets needed
3. **Automatic Creation**: Ready to use as soon as you sign up
4. **Secure**: Only you can authorize transactions

### Your Wallet Address

Your wallet has a unique address (like `0x1234...abcd`). This is your identifier on the blockchain. You can:

- View it in your wallet section
- Share it to receive funds from others
- Use it for QR code payments

## USDC - The Currency

### What is USDC?

USDC is a **stablecoin** - a digital currency that maintains a 1:1 value with the US Dollar.

- 1 USDC = $1 USD (always)
- Fast, low-cost transactions
- Widely accepted in crypto

### Why USDC?

We chose USDC because:
- **Stable Value**: No crypto price volatility
- **Low Fees**: Cheaper than credit card processing
- **Fast**: Transactions settle in seconds
- **Global**: Works anywhere in the world

## The Escrow System

### How It Works

When you book a service:

1. **Payment Locked**: Your USDC is transferred to a smart contract (escrow)
2. **Service Performed**: The stylist completes your appointment
3. **Funds Released**: Payment automatically goes to the stylist

### Why Escrow?

Escrow protects everyone:

**For Customers:**
- Don't pay until service is complete
- Automatic refunds on cancellation
- Dispute resolution available

**For Stylists:**
- Guaranteed payment for completed work
- No chargebacks or payment reversals
- Immediate access to funds

### Escrow States

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Locked** | Funds held during booking |
| **Released** | Paid to stylist on completion |
| **Refunded** | Returned to customer on cancellation |

## Troubleshooting

### "Transaction Failed"

1. Check your balance is sufficient
2. Wait a moment and try again
3. Contact support if issue persists

### "Faucet Unavailable"

- You can only claim once per 24 hours
- Check if you've already claimed today
- Wait until the cooldown period ends

### "Withdrawal Pending"

- Bank transfers take 1-3 business days
- Check your bank details are correct
- Contact support if delayed beyond 5 days
