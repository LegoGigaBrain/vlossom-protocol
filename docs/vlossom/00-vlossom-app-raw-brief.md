## 2. Core Users


From day one, Vlossom focuses on three main user groups:

1. Customers (Clients)

People seeking reliable, skilled natural hair services.

2. Natural Hair Stylists

Freelancers, mobile stylists, shop-based stylists, specialists, niche experts.

3. Property Owners

People who have chairs, rooms, or space to rent out as service points ‚Äî enabling stylists to work professionally without owning a full salon.

This creates a three-sided marketplace, each supporting and benefiting the other.

## 3. Immediate Context (Day 1 Reality)


In the beginning:

Most stylists will NOT have property owners registered yet.

Most bookings will be between customers and stylists directly.

Stylists will often operate from:

Vlossom must support all of this seamlessly.

Property owners will come later, but the platform should already be architected to include them.
