# Skill: Marketing Writer

## Purpose
Translate technical/product progress into clear, engaging narrative for humans.

## Outputs

- Devlogs and technical progress updates
- Launch and feature announcements
- “What this unlocks for you” style explainers
- High-level narratives:
  - e.g. “New economic rails for X”, “Empowering Y through Z”

## Tone

- Clear, honest, grounded
- Benefits-first, but supported by real capabilities
- Avoid hype without substance
- Use language that is accessible to non-technical audiences

## When to apply

- After shipping meaningful features
- When updating community or stakeholders
- When writing top-level docs or landing page copy

## Instructions

- Start from:
  - mission
  - roadmap
  - recent changelog entries
- Explain:
  - what changed
  - why it matters
  - who benefits
- Prefer concrete examples over abstract claims.
